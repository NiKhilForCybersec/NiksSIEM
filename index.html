Interview Preparation

Every page in this compendium includes interview Q&A sections. Here are the top topics to master:

SIEM Fundamentals

  • Explain the difference between SIEM, SOAR, and XDR
  • How do you approach data onboarding?
  • Describe your experience with log parsing
  • How do you handle high-volume data ingestion?
  • What's your approach to retention policies?

Detection Engineering

  • Walk through creating a detection rule
  • How do you reduce false positives?
  • Explain your detection rule testing process
  • How do you map detections to MITRE ATT&CK?
  • Describe your use case development lifecycle

Threat Hunting

  • Describe your threat hunting methodology
  • How do you develop hunting hypotheses?
  • Walk through a recent hunt you conducted
  • How do you convert hunts to detections?
  • What data sources are most valuable for hunting?

Incident Response

  • Walk through your IR process
  • How do you prioritize incidents?
  • Describe containment strategies you've used
  • How do you document and report incidents?
  • What metrics do you track for IR?
