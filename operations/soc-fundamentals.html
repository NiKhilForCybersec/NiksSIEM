SOC Fundamentals

Core concepts and foundational knowledge for Security Operations Center analysts.

SOC Mission

The Security Operations Center is responsible for:

  • Detection: Identify security threats and anomalies
  • Analysis: Investigate and triage alerts
  • Response: Contain and remediate incidents
  • Prevention: Improve security posture proactively

SOC Tiers

Tier 1 - Alert Triage

First responders who monitor alerts and perform initial triage.

Tier 2 - Investigation

Experienced analysts who perform deep-dive investigations.

Tier 3 - Threat Hunting

Senior analysts who proactively hunt for threats and develop detections.
