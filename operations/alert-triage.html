Alert Triage

Systematic approach to triaging security alerts efficiently and effectively.

Triage Process

  1. Initial Assessment: Review alert details and severity
  2. Context Gathering: Check asset criticality, user role
  3. Validation: Confirm if activity is malicious or benign
  4. Escalation: Escalate or close based on findings

Decision Framework

Questions to Ask

  • Is this expected behavior for this user/system?
  • Is the affected asset critical?
  • Are there related alerts or indicators?
  • What is the potential business impact?
