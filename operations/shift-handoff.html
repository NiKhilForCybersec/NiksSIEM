Handoff Best Practices

  • 15-minute overlap: Incoming analyst arrives 15 min early for verbal handoff
  • Document everything: Never rely on verbal only - write it in the handoff log
  • Active incidents first: Prioritize discussing ongoing incidents that need attention
  • System status: Confirm SIEM health, any ingestion issues
  • Context matters: Share anything unusual about the day (maintenance, attacks, etc.)
