Threat Hunting

Proactive threat hunting methodologies and techniques for SOC analysts.

Hunting Approaches

Hypothesis-Driven

Start with a theory about potential threats based on intelligence.

IOC-Based

Search for known indicators of compromise.

Anomaly-Based

Look for deviations from baseline behavior.

TTP-Based

Hunt for specific adversary techniques (ATT&CK mapping).

Hunt Process

  1. Define Hypothesis: What are you looking for?
  2. Identify Data Sources: What logs are needed?
  3. Develop Queries: Create search queries
  4. Execute Hunt: Run queries and analyze results
  5. Document Findings: Record results and create detections
