Interview Preparation

How do you use MITRE ATT&CK in your SOC?

I use ATT&CK in three ways:

1. Coverage Analysis: Map existing detection rules to ATT&CK techniques to identify gaps. This shows me which attack stages I can detect vs. blind spots. I focus on high-frequency techniques first.

2. Detection Development: When building new rules, I start with the ATT&CK technique page - it lists data sources, detection recommendations, and real-world examples. This helps write better detections.

3. Threat Intel Correlation: When reading threat reports, I map adversary TTPs to ATT&CK. This tells me immediately if I have detection for the techniques used in an attack campaign targeting my industry.

I maintain a coverage heatmap and review it quarterly. Target is 80%+ coverage for execution, persistence, and credential access - these are in almost every attack chain.
