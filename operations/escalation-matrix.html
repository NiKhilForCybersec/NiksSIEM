Interview Preparation

When do you escalate an incident vs handling it yourself?

I escalate based on these triggers:

To Tier 2: When I need deeper forensic analysis, can't determine malicious vs benign within 30 minutes, see indicators of lateral movement, or the attack technique is beyond my expertise.

To IR Team: Confirmed active intrusion, ransomware, evidence of data exfiltration, compromised privileged accounts, or anything requiring containment decisions.

To Management: Business impact expected, legal/compliance implications, or need for executive communication.

The key principle: when in doubt, escalate. It's better to over-escalate than to sit on a critical incident. I always document why I'm escalating and what specific help I need.
