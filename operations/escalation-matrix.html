When to Escalate

Immediate Escalation Triggers

  • Ransomware encryption detected
  • Confirmed data exfiltration
  • Executive account compromise
  • Critical infrastructure affected
  • Multiple systems compromised (lateral movement)
  • Attacker actively present in environment
  • Regulatory data exposed (PII, PCI, HIPAA)

Interview Questions

Escalate when:

  • Incident exceeds your authority/capability to handle
  • Business impact requires management awareness
  • Regulatory/legal implications exist
  • You need resources beyond your access
  • Time-sensitive and you can't reach resolution quickly

Key principle: When in doubt, escalate. It's better to over-communicate than miss a critical incident.
