Risk-Based Alerting

15%

RBA aggregates risk scores to reduce alert fatigue and prioritize threats.

ComponentPurpose
Risk ObjectEntity being assessed (user, host, IP)
Risk ScoreNumeric severity (1-100+)
Risk IndexStores risk events
Risk NotablesAlerts when threshold exceeded
