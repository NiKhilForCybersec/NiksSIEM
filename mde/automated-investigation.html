Automated Investigation & Response

Leverage AIR capabilities in Microsoft Defender for Endpoint for automatic threat remediation.

How AIR Works

Automated Investigation and Response (AIR) automatically investigates alerts and takes remediation actions.

Investigation Steps

  1. Alert triggers investigation
  2. Entities analyzed (files, processes, services)
  3. Verdicts determined (malicious/suspicious/clean)
  4. Remediation actions proposed or executed

Automation Levels

  • Full: All remediation automatic
  • Semi: Requires approval for some actions
  • No automation: Manual approval for all
