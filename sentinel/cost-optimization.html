Cost Optimization

Optimize Microsoft Sentinel costs through efficient data management and commitment tiers.

Pricing Model

Pay-As-You-Go vs Commitment Tiers

  • Pay-As-You-Go: ~$2.46/GB ingested
  • 100 GB/day: ~$1.96/GB (20% savings)
  • 200 GB/day: ~$1.72/GB (30% savings)
  • 500 GB/day: ~$1.48/GB (40% savings)

Cost Reduction Strategies

Data Filtering

  • Use transformation rules to drop unnecessary fields
  • Filter verbose logs at collection time
  • Use Basic Logs for low-value high-volume data

Retention Optimization

  • Interactive retention: 90 days (default)
  • Archive tier: 2+ years at lower cost
  • Per-table retention policies
