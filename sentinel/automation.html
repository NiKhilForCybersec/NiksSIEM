Automation & Playbooks

Automation in Sentinel uses Logic Apps to create playbooks that respond to alerts and incidents automatically. From enrichment to containment to notification - automation is how you scale your SOC.

What are Playbooks?

Playbooks are Logic Apps workflows triggered by Sentinel alerts or incidents. They can enrich alerts with threat intelligence, contain threats by blocking IPs or disabling users, create tickets, send notifications, and much more.

Common Playbook Use Cases

Enrichment

Add threat intel, WHOIS, GeoIP, VirusTotal reputation to alerts before analyst review

Containment

Block IPs in firewall, disable compromised accounts, isolate endpoints via Defender

Notification

Send Teams/Slack messages, emails, SMS for critical alerts. Page on-call teams.

Ticketing

Create ServiceNow, Jira, or Zendesk tickets with incident details automatically

Interview Questions

Alert trigger:

  • Immediate automated response needed
  • Enrichment before incident grouping
  • High-confidence detections requiring instant containment

Incident trigger:

  • Need all related alerts in context
  • Notification workflows (single alert per incident)
  • Ticket creation (one ticket per incident, not per alert)
